DLC Boss Sound Effects — Required List

SHORT (≤ 1 second)

shadow_step_vanish — dark whoosh/pop as boss teleports out
shadow_step_reappear — low thud + energy crack on reappear
shadow_trail_tick — subtle pulse/hiss (spawned every 3 frames in phase 2+)
shadow_fan_shot — multi-projectile whoosh (5–7 shots fired in burst)
shadow_regular_shot — single aimed dark bolt
Mimic King (Faith of Fortune)

wheel_tick — mechanical ratchet click (played each tick during spin deceleration)
wheel_land — satisfying "clunk" when wheel stops on a result
mimic_aimed_burst_shot — fast cracking bolt (×5 in quick succession)
mimic_copy_hit — mirror shatter/resonance sting (on COPY ATTACK fire)
gambit_jackpot — slot machine win chime (40% outcome)
gambit_nothing — comedic fart/fail stinger (60% outcome — "HA! NOTHING!")
mimic_decoy_shot — dim, muffled version of regular shot for decoys
mimic_regular_shot — standard gold bolt fire
mimic_scatter_drop — each individual slow orb dropping (×18)
Cloud Golem (Waker of Winds)

gust_push — sharp short wind burst
hailstone_impact — single ice pellet crack (plays ×8 staggered)
Storm Crow (Waker of Winds)

screech_land — sharp bird-of-prey screech on dive arrival
crow_wing_flap — single wing beat (used as ambient tick while hovering)
Tornado Machina (Waker of Winds)

tornado_projectile_spawn — mechanical clicking wind-up tick
Tempest (Waker of Winds)

tempest_shot — fast purple energy bolt
lightning_arc_crackle — short electrical spark (ambient, fires every 4 frames)

thunder_spear_launch — fast electric snap/crack (fired after 0.5s delay)

zeus_teleport_flash — electric pop/snap on dash teleport
