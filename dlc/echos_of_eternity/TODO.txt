# Echos of Eternity DLC

This DLC introduces a new fundamental dimension: Time, alongside a hidden, mysterious counterpart: Love, expanding the game into philosophical, narrative, and mechanical depth never seen before.

## Vision

This DLC represents the narrative and mechanical culmination of the entire game. It should also be like a "best of" of the base game and all DLCs.

It must feel:
- Larger in scale than all previous DLCs
- More complex, but still understandable
- Deeply connected to everything that came before

“Echoes of Eternity” is not just another DLC.
It is the moment where all timelines converge,
where every decision matters,
and where the unseen force of connection proves stronger than fate itself.

It is also a deeply personal DLC for the developer and as the last DLC, it should really round up the whole game.

## Heroes

### New Hero: Time (Sand-Colored Hero)

Time is a control-oriented, reality-bending hero who manipulates the flow of time, rewinds events, accelerates outcomes, and creates temporal echoes of actions.
He represents inevitability, causality, and the burden of knowing what will happen.
Unlike other heroes, Time does not simply fight enemies —
he reshapes the battlefield itself across multiple timelines simultaneously.

Core Gameplay Identity:
Battlefield manipulation across past, present, and future
Creates temporal clones (“echoes”) of previous actions
Can rewind damage, reposition enemies, or undo mistakes
Strong strategic depth, but high complexity

Unique Special Ability: Timeline Fracture
The battlefield splits into parallel timelines
Actions in one timeline affect others
Players must manage multiple realities at once

Unique Level up mechanic: Fast-forward or reverse

There are only two level-up options (except at waves that are multiples of 10 with the Ultimate option):

- Fast forward: Buffs all attributes of the hero, making him stronger in any sense
- Reverse: Weekens current and resolves timeline fracture to handle all the mess

Idea: The player must balance between being able to handle the overwhelming timeline chaos and getting stronger. A skilled player can just keep on getting stronger and stronger, while players that can't handle the chaos will remain weak.

Ultimate Form:
“Eternal Paradox”
Time freezes, then collapses multiple timelines into one
All accumulated actions resolve simultaneously

### Hidden Hero: Love (Pink Hero)

Love is a hidden, unlockable hero that represents connection, empathy, and emotional resonance.
She is not initially playable and only becomes available through specific narrative conditions tied to Time and the main story.
Love is neither purely offensive nor defensive —
she binds entities together, manipulates emotional states, and alters relationships between enemies, allies, and even reality itself.

Core Gameplay Identity:
Links enemies and allies through emotional bonds
Converts damage, healing, and effects between linked entities
Can pacify enemies, but also amplify conflict if misused

Idea:

- Easy hero
- Crazy power fantasy
- Can seduce enemies to no attack any more
- Can spawn a companion
- Strong “heart” attacks that blast through enemies

Unique Mechanic: Emotional Resonance
Enemies and allies become linked
Effects propagate across connections:
- Damage spreads
- Healing shares
- Status effects transfer
- Ultimate Form:
- “Heart of Unity”
All entities on the battlefield become interconnected
Massive chain reactions of effects (both positive and negative)

## Story mechanics

Time Story:

- New meta mechanism let’s you chose one of alternative paths after every wave in the "Maze of Time"
    - Goal: Finish up all alternative paths (that are built up like a maze/tree structure) across various playthroughs to get to the real endings
    - One playthrough will only lead you through one strang, but some events require certain, sometimes alternative events to be completed across multiple playthroughs
    - That way a web is created
    - Only when all events are cleared, you can select the map node that leads to the fight of the final showdown and superboss
    - That means it's possible to finish the story on a path that doesn't lead to the final boss, every run can be completely different depending on the strang that you decide to pass through
- One of the later alternative paths lead to discovering the Love hero
- The paths go through the whole story of the game and discovers different paths and shows what happens before and after the story
- Special paths include for example:
    - One that leads to the true golden mask
    - One where he has Black as companion
    - One where he has to fight Poison and Sound
    - One where he fights Makuta and Green Goblin
- Paths should be like the ultimate playground with crazy outcomes and variations (e.g. only one enemy type but super strong, or play around with biomes, weather, challenges to complete...all currently known story modifiers and more are possible)
- Paths that were already taken are marked as such in the map
- The map is a new mechanic. It opens after a wave is completed and there the player choses where to go next.
- Certain paths lead to formidable foes, special bosses that are on the hunting list
    - those are special, new boss implementations
- The hunting list can be seen after every wave completion, together with the map, and does not reset after every game run
- Finishing foes on the hunting list brings player buffs. Finishing all of them brings a special super buff for runs in that campaign.
- Paths in the map are revealed when choosing them, but marked with “?” if not discovered yet
- All path nodes have an icon depicting the kind of event and visually indicate if they have been completed yet
- The map state always carries over to future campaign runs
- Every node on the map represents a story chapter that is also narrated, so in this case we need to change the way the story chapters work (there is no fixed order now!)
- In total I was thinking about ~70-100 unique nodes in the overall map

Love Story:

- She shares the story with the Time hero
- Allows for some alternative paths in the Maze of Time that only she can take

Time hero:

Love hero:

## DLC Content Overview

As with all heroes, Time and Love feature:

- Unique, deeply thematic skill trees
- A shared but perspective-shifting storyline
- This DLC introduces cross-timeline interactions between:
    - All previous DLC heroes
    - Major villains (Makuta, Zeus, Tempest, Entropy Lord, etc.)
    - The mysterious Black hero

## Unique Biomes
### Time Biome – The Shattered Continuum
Floating fragments of different timelines
Environments constantly shifting between states
Special mechanics:
Time loops
Repeating enemy patterns
Delayed or reversed effects

### Love Biome – The Heart Nexus (Hidden)
Ethereal, glowing environments
Calm but emotionally intense atmosphere
Special mechanics:
Linked enemies and allies
Emotional field effects
Non-lethal encounter solutions

## Skill Trees
### Time Skill Tree (Example Branches)
Path of Reversal: Rewind abilities, undo damage
Path of Acceleration: Faster attacks, compressed timelines
Path of Paradox: High-risk timeline manipulation

### Love Skill Tree (Example Branches)
Path of Connection: Stronger links between entities
Path of Compassion: Healing and protection effects
Path of Passion: Amplified effects with higher risk
Altar of Mastery & Mutations

This DLC introduces:
Unique Altar of Mastery skills for Time and Love
Convergence skills with ALL existing elements
Examples:
Time + Lightning: Delayed chain lightning strikes across timelines
Time + Gravity: Time dilation fields
Love + Fire: Shared burning effects across linked enemies
Love + Void: Emotional instability causing unpredictable effects
Love + Plant: Growth-based healing networks
